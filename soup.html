Calandracas

CREDITS TO : Ang Sarap

Traditionally made with a base of vegetables, potatoes, and chickpeas, this dish welcomes a variety of proteins, from chicken and pork to beef and even ham. The inclusion of either sotanghon noodles or pasta adds another layer of texture, making it a truly satisfying meal.




Mutya ng Cavite

CREDITS TO : Ang Sarap

This rich and creamy soup lives up to its name, offering a luxurious combination of fresh seafood. Imagine plump mussels, succulent prawns, and flaky fish, all simmered in a luscious cream sauce.
