Chicharon Bulaklak

CREDITS TO : Yummy.PH

Cavite's chicharon bulaklak belies its floral name. This unique appetizer isn't made from flowers, but rather from a surprising source: pig mesentery. The mesentery, the fatty tissue that holds the pig's intestines in place, is transformed into a delightful treat. Deep-fried until golden brown and crispy, chicharon bulaklak offers a satisfying crunch with a hint of porky flavor.

Lawlaw

CREDITS TO : RunningPinoy

Lawlaw, a unique fish found in the coastal regions of Cavite, is a key player in the "Taste Cavite" lunch experience. This versatile catch can be enjoyed in a few ways. Fried to a crisp, its flavor profile is said to resemble the beloved Taal Tawilis, a type of dried fish. This crispy version offers a delightful alternative to chips, perfect for snacking.
